About Pacific Corporate Security

Who We Are

Pacific Corporate Security Ltd (PCS) is a 100% Papua New Guinea owned company, providing professional security services across the country since 2004. We are committed to delivering reliable, high-quality protection services tailored to meet the needs of our clients.

Our Mission

To provide professional and reliable security services through highly trained personnel and modern security solutions, ensuring safety and peace of mind.

Our Vision

To be the leading security provider in Papua New Guinea, recognized for excellence, trust, and commitment to client satisfaction.

Leadership

Under the leadership of our Managing Director, PCS has grown into a trusted security provider with over 20 years of experience in the industry. Our management team is dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of service and operational excellence.
