WHO WE ARE

Pacific Corporate Security Ltd (PCS) is a 100% Papua New Guinea owned company, providing professional and reliable security services nationwide. Since 2004, PCS has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality protection solutions tailored to meet the needs of businesses and individuals.

Our strength lies in our highly trained personnel, experienced management team, and commitment to excellence in every operation we undertake.
